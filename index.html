During World War II, Lt. Gen. Leslie Groves Jr. appoints physicist
J. Robert Oppenheimer to work on the top-secret Manhattan Project.
Oppenheimer and a team of scientists spend years developing and
designing the atomic bomb. Their work comes to fruition on July 16, 1945,
as they witness the world's fiirst nuclear explosion, forever changing the
course of history. The movie follows the story of the American scientist,
Oppenheimer, and his role in the development of the atomic bomb.
